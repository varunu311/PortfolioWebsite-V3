CHARE

Transform your daily stops into acts of kindness with CHARE! Every time you make a transaction at a location, our app effortlessly rounds up your payment to the nearest dollar. The spare change then goes directly to a deserving charity, chosen at random. Seamlessly integrate giving into your daily routine and watch as your small change makes big differences.
